Academics

At Future Life Academy and Junior School, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive and dynamic education. Our curriculum follows the CBC (Competency-Based Curriculum) framework, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and emotional intelligence.

Our Academic Programs

ECDE

Our ECDE program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and character development. It is designed to ignite a bright future for all!

Primary School

We use a designed curriculum to prepare students for a future of success, both in academic excellence, co-curricular activities, and leadership development.

Junior School

Subjects Offered

Our curriculum includes a wide variety of subjects to ensure a well-rounded education. The following subjects are part of our academic offerings:

  • English Language & Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Studies
  • ICT (Information and Communication Technology)
  • Creative Arts (Music, Art, Drama)
  • Physical Education
  • Religious Education
  • Environmental Studies
